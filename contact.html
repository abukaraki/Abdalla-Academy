When to Contact Us

  • Suggest a new lesson or article.
  • Report a broken link or information that needs an update.
  • Ask about the privacy policy or use of content.
  • Request removal of content you own or believe was used without permission.

Response Time

We try to review messages and requests within a reasonable time. When sending a request, please include the relevant page or link so it can be reviewed quickly.
